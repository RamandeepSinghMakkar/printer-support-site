Understanding Your Printer

Modern printers come equipped with advanced wireless capabilities, high-resolution scanning, and efficient ink management systems. Ensuring you have the correct specifications is vital for optimal performance.

Key Features to Look For:

  • Wireless Print & Scan
  • High Yield Ink Compatibility
  • Auto Duplex Printing
  • Cloud Print Ready

Maintenance Tips

Regular maintenance ensures longevity. Keep the print heads clean, use quality paper, and ensure firmware is up to date.
